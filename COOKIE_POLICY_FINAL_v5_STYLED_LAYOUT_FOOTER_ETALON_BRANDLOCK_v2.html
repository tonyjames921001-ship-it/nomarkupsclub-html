Cookie Policy
A clear explanation of how cookies and similar technologies are used on this site.
Updated

We highly value your privacy and are committed to keeping your personal information secure and confidential at all times. If you have any questions or concerns regarding how we handle your data, our customer support team is available to assist you.

While shopping on our website, you may be asked to provide details such as your name, email, business or shipping address, phone number, payment details, and password.

We also keep records of your preferences and past orders. This helps us offer you a smoother experience, allowing you to track orders and revisit items you’ve browsed or purchased. Cookies are used to save data related to your activity on the site—such as your cart and wishlist.

Through cookies and data from your browser, we and certain third-party services may gather additional technical information like your IP address, browser type, location, pages visited, and search queries used on our website.

We collect this data to:
• Process transactions and deliver services;
• Analyze usage patterns to improve the site;
• Provide relevant content and offers;
• Run promotional activities and prize draws;
• Manage the functionality of our website;
• And—only with your consent—send updates about new products or special deals.

Your data may be shared with trusted partners solely to help complete your purchase. These partners are required to maintain strict confidentiality.

When you register an account, you’ll have the option to subscribe to our mailing list. If you provide your contact details by entering promotions or signing up, we may use that information to send newsletters and updates about exclusive deals or new arrivals. You can unsubscribe at any time by following the opt-out link in our emails.

 

Security Notice:
Our website uses 128-bit SSL encryption to protect your data during transmission. All payments are securely processed by PayPal—your card information is never stored on our servers. Additionally, we take great care to keep your account password private and secure.

Tip: You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.
